Bacon Lettuce Tomato

Description

Need a quick portable lunch? The BLT is exactly what you're looking for!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 1 leaf of lettuce
  • 2 slice of bread
  • 4 tomato slices
  • salt as needed
  • your preferred sauce

Steps:

  • Cook your bacon on pan.
  • Toast your bread, either on a pan or with a toaster.
  • Season your vegetables lightly with salt.
  • Cut the bacon and veggies to fit the size of your bread.

    • It's time to assemble your sandwich!

  • Spread your choice of sauce onto both sides, then add your other ingredients.

    • Are you by any chance a fan of mayo just like us? Check out our mayo recipe.
