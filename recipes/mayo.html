Mayonnaise

Description

Colloquially known as mayo, this is the most simple and popular sauce in the world!

We will teach you how to make it in the comfort of your own home!

Ingredients:

  • 2 egg yolks
  • 250g seed oil
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon of mustard
  • salt as needed

Steps:

  1. Separate your egg yolks from your egg whites.
  2. Then, into your food processor, add your egg yolks.
  3. Start blitzing the egg yolks while slowly drizzling the oil.

    4. It is important that you do this slowly, or you might split the sauce entirely.

  4. Once you've added about half of your oil, it's time to add the juice from your lemon and a teaspoon of mustard.

    Add your new ingredients slowly, just as we did with the oil.

  5. Once we've reached the right consistency, it's time to season with salt and voil√†!

    6. Be mindful to consume your mayo on the spot. You can store it in the fridge, but you should consume it within the next 2 days.
